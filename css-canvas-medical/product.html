It's the foundation for patient-
centered primary care.

Canvas creates a shared understanding of patient care across clinicians,
staff, and patients themselves -- making you more productive, less
stressed, and adding just a bit more joy into your practice of medicine.

It puts care at your command

You don't have to click a dozen times to order a flu shot or bounce around five windows to discover the last blood count. Canvas pulls everything you need directly into your note, so you never have to leave it. And because Canvas learns from every interaction, it gets smarter with every patient you see.

Learn more about charting.

It brings your population to life

Canvas makes it simple to search and take action across your entire patient panel, so you know who needs extra help and how best to give it to them. Easily reach out to any subgroup of your population with proactive interventions, and take comfort knowing you're a step ahead.

Learn more about population management.

It automates performance improvement

Clinical quality and revenue cycle reports can help you understand performance, but they aren't enough to improve quality and increase revenue day after day. Canvas automates quality protocols and charge capture so clinicians can focus on more complex work only they can do.
